\newcommandx{\bu}[2][2=i]{\frac{W_{#2}-#1(f_{#2}(\bmin[#2])-f_{#2}(\bmax[#2]))+r_{#2}f_{#2}(\bmax[#2])}

\newcommandx{\bd}[2][2=i]{\frac{W_{#2}-f_{#2}(\bmin[#2])d_{#2}+#1f_{#2}(\bmax[#2])}

\newcommandx{\bup}[2][2=j]{\frac{W_{#2}-#1f_{#2}(\bmin[#2])+r_{#2}f_{#2}(\bmax[#2])}

\newcommandx{\bdp}[2][2=j]{\frac{#1(f_{#2}(\bmin[#2])-f_{#2}(\bmax[#2]))+d_{#2}f_{#2}(\bmax[#2])-W_{#2}}{f_{#2}(\bmin[#2])}}

si $\bdp{d_i} \le \emin$ et $(d_i \ge \emin[j]$ ou $d_i \le \htd)$ et $d_j \ge d_i \ge \smax[j]\ \longrightarrow\ \inter[\bdp{d_i}][d_i]$\\
